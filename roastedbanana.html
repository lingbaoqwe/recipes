Roasted Banana

Roasted bananas are great over ice cream, sprinkled with cinnamon-sugar, in a smoothie, or straight out of the air fryer. Made with only 2 ingredients and ready in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, sliced into 1/8-inch thick diagonals
  • avocado oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Line air fryer basket with parchment paper.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Place banana slices into the basket, making sure that they are not touching; cook in batches if necessary. Mist banana slices with avocado oil.
  4. Cook in the air fryer for 5 minuets. Remove basket and flip banana slices carefully (they will be soft). Cook until banana slices are browning and caramelized, an additional 2 to 3 minutes. Carefully remove from basket.
